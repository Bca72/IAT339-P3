About Me

Hey! I am Benson Chow

I am a SIAT (School of Interactive Arts and Technology) major studying at Simon Fraser University. Ever since I was a kid, I've been captivated by the power of technology to shape and enhance our lives. This passion for all things digital led me to pursue a degree in SIAT, where I've been able to explore the intersection of design and development. My mission as a developer is to craft simple and clean interfaces with seamless interactions. Whether it's for a website, mobile app, or an interactive installation.
